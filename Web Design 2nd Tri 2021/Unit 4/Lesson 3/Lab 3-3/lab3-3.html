Beowulf Blues

To say that Beowulf was a hero would be an under-statement. Not only did he show love for his hometown, but also for those he fought with.

But was his love for his worries really the same towards him? Sure they fought for him and proclaimed their loyalty to him, but could the warrior’s outrageous boasting be the cause of the end of Beowulf?

From a report we received yesterday, Beowulf died fighting an oversized dragon while his comrades ran in terror. His name is Enkidu, and he is the rightful heir to the throne. His coronation will be next Wednesday at noon.

A Sport of a Different Sort

The Native Anglo-Saxon Association (N.A.S.A) has created a new sport for the teens of Denmark. This new sport involves a catapult and a fresh pack of chicken meat. The point of the game is to load the chicken meat in to the catapult as fast as you can, and then launch it at an opposing enemy.

The first team to hit the opposing team with 5 fresh packs of chicken meat wins the trophy and is allowed to take the meat back to the hometown.

For more information on the rules of this game, please talk to the herot.
