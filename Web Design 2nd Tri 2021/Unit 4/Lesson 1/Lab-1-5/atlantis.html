Shuttle Atlantis

The Shuttle Atlantis flew 32 mission, including 7 to the former Soviet / Russian space station Mir. In 1989, Atlantis deployed the planetary probes Magellan to Venus and Galileo to Jupiter. In addition the Space Shuttle Atlantis flew multiple missions to the International Space Station and the Hubble Space Telescope. When the Space Shuttle Program was canceled the shuttle Atlantis was to be used for parts following its final mission, however it continued to fly and was the last space shuttle to fly and ending the program upon its landing. The shuttle was retired and is on display at the Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex in Florida.
