Shuttle Discovery

The Shuttle Discovery made its first flight on August 30. With the loss of Columbia, Discovery is known as the fleet leader with the most flights of 39 missions traveling over 149 million miles. Discovery is also the most photographed shuttle as it was the return-to-flight shuttle following both the Challenger and Columbia disasters. The Shuttle Discovery is currently on display at the Smithsonian in Washington DC.
