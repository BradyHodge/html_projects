Shuttle Endeavour

The Shuttle Endeavour's first mission was to capture and redeploy a stranded communications satellite. Since then the shuttle made numerous missions to the International Space Station and to the Hubble. Endeavour also transported the first African-American woman into space on its second mission. Endeavour's final mission was to the International Space Station to deliver hardware after which it was officially decommissioned. The shuttle is now on display at the California Science Center in Los Angeles.
