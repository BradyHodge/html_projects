Do:

  • Dress smartly but simply
  • Be well groomed
  • Go to the interview alone
  • Arrive 15 minutes early
  • Bring paper, pen, resume, and social security number
  • Greet the interviewer by name
  • Remaining standing until you are asked to sit down
  • Maintain eye contact
  • Be honest and straightforward
  • Answer all questions briefly but completely
  • Ask questions about the decision
  • Thank the interviewer for their time
  • Ask when a decision will be made

Don't:

  • Carry packages with you
  • Chew gum
  • Wear sunglasses
  • Talk against your former employer or teachers
